Tuesday, June 16, 2021

10:15-12:45am (Online Event)

JC BOSE UNIVERSITY OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY

Sector-6 , YMCA chowk , Mathura road , Haryana

Organisers:

    • Dr. Parul Tomar, Convenor, IIC shared that total 150 students from different courses and disciplines along with 25 Faculty members are currently part of IIC. She also mentioned that Institution’s Innovation Council (IIC) is an Initiative of Ministry of Education Innovation cell with the purpose of exploring more opportunities for students and Faculty members as well.
    • Dr. Sapna Gambhir, Vice President IIC, added that under the umbrella of IIC, four verticals named Incubation, Innovation, Start-up and IPR are coordinated by Mr. Ajay Sharma, Ms. Rashmi Chawla, Dr. Rashmi Popli and Dr. Ashlesha Gupta along with their teams of students and other faculty members. For smooth conduct of activities, a student core committee of approx. 20 students is also constituted.
    • Prof. Lakwinder Singh, President IIC introduced all faculty members and also mentioned that at least one faculty member, from each department of the University, is part of IIC for fostering innovation culture in the University. He mentioned that Mr. Suneel Wattal, Mr. Anil Breja, Mr. Manish Pradhan, Mr. Sumit Jain are external members of IIC who are always keen to mentor students on various grounds and share their knowledge and experiences during quarterly meetings.
